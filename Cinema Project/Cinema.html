About Academy Cinemas

Located in the sun-filled city of Tampa, Florida.

Our reels have been spinning since 1960. Showing our guests a great time is our passion and seeing their smiles our pleasure!

Accommodations

All of our auditoriums are equipped with brand new christie 3d® sound systems!

Relax in our, extra-wide, plush cushioning reclines. Seating is on first-come-first-served basis. Our seating arrangment is disability friendly ♿.

Our auditoriums are climate controlled to approximatley 73° year-round.
